Model.Game Types

Abstract games are the ones who offer no theme, or if there is one, it is not part from the actual experience of playing. Chess is a world wide known game of this genre.

Economic simulation games are trying to simulate a level of economy, good for teaching kids about how money works from a young age. The most popular game of this genre is Monopoly.

Paper-and-pencil games are the ones the you would play during class with your deskmate, when you're bored. The most popular ones are tic-tac-toe and hangman.
