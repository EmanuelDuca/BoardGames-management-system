Our recent events

If you would like to become a member of our team, please send us an email, containing the reason why you love board games and the name of your favourite one.
We welcome everyone in our team, even if you are totally new in the world of board games. Don't be shy, you don't have to master all games, just be open and friendly. Board games are a great opportunity to socialize with others and to make new friends.

If you are interested in another events connected to VIA, you can for example take part in jam sessions at Platform K free of charge from the 1st of October, but VIA has so much more for you to join...
